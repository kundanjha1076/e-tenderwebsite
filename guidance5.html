Manual for Online Vendor Payment Tracking

  1. Open BEML Website on Internet Explorer Browser only and Click on “Suppliers – Know Your Bill Status”




  2. Once SRM Login page is opened, Enter User ID and Password and Click on “Log On”




  3. Once Logged onto SRM Portal, Click on “Reports”




  4. Click on “Vendor Payment Tracking”




  5. Enter Purchase Order No. (Mandatory Field) and click on “Submit”. Entering Invoice Number is optional.




  6. Status of payments for the items supplied against the PO is displayed as a table.
