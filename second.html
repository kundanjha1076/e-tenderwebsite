HOW TO SEARCH FOR TENDER AND DOWNLOAD DOCUMENTS



  • login -> username & password
  • Refresh.
  • Tender list will be displayed below.
  • For searching (display quick critiria mentainance):
    1. keep the event status blank, enter the event number (tender number) and click on apply.
    2. Click on apply for finding specific tender.(if its a 2 bit tender then click on Technical Rf'x ),(if its not 2 bit tender and a normal one then you can find it in the Notes and Attachments.
  • click on Technical RF'x.
  • For first timers,a pop up box will appear. (click accept and next show content).
  • Next page will be loaded in the section of CURRENT VERSION below the documnets will be found , right click on the documents and set path to download the files.
  • Logout from the cFolder.
  • Tender documents are in the 1 bit then you can find them on the Notes and Attachments.
