Manual for Viewing Price Details of Bids Received from Other Vendors

  1. Open website BEML in Internet Explorer Browser only and Click on “Suppliers – eProcurement (SRM)”




  2. Once SRM Login page is opened, Enter User ID and Password and Click on “Log On”




  3. Once Logged onto SRM Portal, Click on “Reports”




  4. Click on “Price Details” to view the Price bids of other vendors after the Bids are opened and decrypted.




  5. Enter Bid Invitation Number (Mandatory Field) and Bid Submission Response (Quotation) Number (Mandatory Field)) and click on “Execute”. .




  6. If the Tender number and Quotation number match, details of Price Bids of all the bids submitted by other vendors against the Tender will be displayed as below. .
