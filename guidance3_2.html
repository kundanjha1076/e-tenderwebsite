HOW TO SUBMIT SRM SINGLE BID TENDER?

  • Login with your user id & password, after setting up the enitre process.
  • Search for the documents that you want to bid.
  • Click on register option than click on RESPONSES
  • If the tender has limit then gets directed from the register form.
  • Window will be opened, click on terms of Payment:
  • New window will open, click on the Search Option:
  • Select your payment option.
  • Click on icon, for delivery to the BEML location do select "FOR" or type your location.
  • Go to Price Condition option:
  • Set your price according to your bidding value.
  • For any extra information regarding the tender, click on the option available on top , price condition option i.e. , Note and Price Attachments
  • Add your extra information ADD in the note section & for adding file , click on attachments in the attachments section
  • For tenders with no price condition , fill the details in the items selected.
  • Go to check option for checking the details.
  • Save the tender or can be submitted later too.
  • Submit the digital signature.
  • The bid java file will run & the file running option is completed. pop up window comes up requesting to run the java file
  • Click on "Encrypt Bid" .
  • The saved tender can be submitted by clicking on the edit option.
  • Select your bid file & click on decription option
  • Window pops up "Enter the smart card number"
  • Enter your digital signature passwordIn class 3 form .
  • Submit the bid after editing price
  • Window pops up named "Digital Signature"
  • Select the correct license token if there are many then proceed to sign option
  • For entering the password of smart card , window pops up for entering the password.
  • Next window pops up (describing about message from webpage)
  • Come back to ensure your status as submitted shown on the top
  • You can close your previous window by clickng on above option which redirects you to main page.
  • Refresh the column tab of bidding list to check your bid.
  • If you want the above procedure , this is the entire process for bidding a single bid, in the bid list page you can find your bid and start editing and resubmit it
